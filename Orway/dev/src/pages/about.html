About Us

Orway is a biometric tech company that creates identification technology solutions for applications that other companies failed. Its history began from development of the first proprietary fingerprint scanners in 1994. Its experience embraces development of several generations of fingerprint identification technology and different products for physical access control, Civil ID and Law Enforcement applications.

A key characteristic of Orway is the presence of all competencies to build biometrics systems of any level. Together with unique scientific findings, technology solutions and know-how it enables Orway to create products with unmatched characteristics and quality while keeping costs very low.
